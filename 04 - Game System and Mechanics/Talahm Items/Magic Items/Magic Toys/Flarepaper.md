### Flarepaper
_Magic Toy, Common_

**Weight:** --  
**Price:** 5 Sticks

A cylinder lined on the inside with gunpowder, and a magical filigree to activate it, and force the flame to shoot out and change color. Resembles a paper tube with a break point 4/5 to the top, when snapped, shoots out a fiery shot up to 15 feet and explodes meant to be pointed at the air
