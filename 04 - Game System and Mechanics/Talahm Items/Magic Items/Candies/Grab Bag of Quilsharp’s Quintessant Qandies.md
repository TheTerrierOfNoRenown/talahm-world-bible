### Grab Bag of Quilsharpe's Quintessant Qandies
*wonderous item*, *common*

**Weight:** 0.5 lbs
**Price:** 7 Sticks

A bag of magical candies that allow the eater to immediately cast a single spell. Sold in grab bags of 5 with a random assortment of delicious treats. Roll on QQQ table when selecting a candy from the bag, to determine the kind of candy you receive.

| D8  | Flavor              | Effect           |
| :-: | ------------------- | ---------------- |
|  1  | Lemon Head          | Dancing Lights   |
|  2  | Green Tea           | Message          |
|  3  | Mint                | Ray of Frost     |
|  4  | Cinnamon Hard Candy | Firebot          |
|  5  | Pop Rocks           | Shocking Grasp   |
|  6  | Super Sour Melon    | Mage Hand        |
|  7  | Caramel Hard Candy  | Friends          |
|  8  | Chocolate Truffle   | Prestidigitation |
