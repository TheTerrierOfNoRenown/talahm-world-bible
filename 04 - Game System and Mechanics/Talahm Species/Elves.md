**While dwarves, and gnomes live long lives, nobody know the true depth of time quite like an Elf. There is no known upper limit to the Age Elves can grow, and it is suspected outside of accidents and murder that Elves can live indefinitely. There are tales of Elves old enough to have greeted mankind leaving the caves and building civilizations, but such tales are speculation. The Elven population is extremely limited after the destruction of its Capital In the Shattering of Colomb Bith 500 years ago. The total number of Elves is estimated to be 1300.**
 
**A Legacy At Its End.** The time of Elves has come to an end. It ended 500 years ago with the shattering of Colomb Bith. Elves legacy is not waning, it has past, and the remaining elves know that. How they react to this knowledge varies from Elf to Elf, some live in denial, some have become happy to see the world moving on without them, some are mournful of its loss. In a way, Elves are a ghost of a worldly order that used to be.
 
**A Dying Population.** While Elves theoretically live eternally, as a species the Elven population would be unsustainable if they all lived in the same place, which they rarely do. There are approximately 1500 Elves in existence, to the extent their presence is appreciably rare.
 
**True Eternity.** There is no known upper limit to the age that an Elf can live to, and indeed there are a few that are so old that keeping track has long lost meaning to them. This affords elves a unique perspective on the world, as save for being killed, Elves do not face mortality like their peers.
 
**Creature Type:** Humanoid  
**Size:** Medium (about 5-6 feet tall  
**Speed:** 30 feet  
As an Elf, you have these special traits  
**Darkvision.** You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet  
**Elven Lineage.** You are part of a lineage that grants you supernatural abilities. Choose a lineage from the Elven Lineages table. You gain the level 1 benefit of that lineage.  
When you reach character levels 3 and 5, you learn a higher-level spell, as shown on the table. You always have that spell prepared. You can cast it once without a spell slot, and you regain the ability to cast it in that way when you finish a Long Rest. You can also cast the spell using any spell slots you have of the appropriate level.
Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait (choose the ability when you select the lineage).
**Ancient View.** As an elf you are aware of the passage of time, but as mostly a theoretical. Because of this you are less concerned with stressful facts about the universe, and therefor less plaible. Advantage against fear and persuasion checks.
**Keen Senses.** You have proficiency in the Insight, Perception, or Survival skill.
**Trance.** You don't need to sleep, and magic can't put you to sleep. You can finish a Long Rest in 4 hours if you spend those hours in a trancelike meditation, during which you retain consciousness.
