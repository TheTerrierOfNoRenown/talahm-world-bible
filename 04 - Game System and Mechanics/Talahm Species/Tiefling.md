**A nomadic people, who worship a strange animistic religion, Tiefling never really belong anywhere. Often mistrusted as perpetual outsiders, there is no shortage of myths and bigotries about Tiefling. Owing to their appearance and general bad reputation, a tribe of Tiefling is often met in cities with scorn. This leaves Tiefling equally mistrusting of others as Good Will is often a limited and pricy resource. While Tiefling are often considered a singular entity by the outside, within are three distinct tribes: The Mystics, The Aurora, and the Carnivale.**
 
## **Tribes**
 
**Mystics**  
The Mystic tribe is strongly invested in the Arcane. Often having soothsayers and Tarot Card readers, Mystics believe strongly in fate and the need to read it.
 
**The Aurora**  
The Aurora tribe is most invested in the strange animistic religion of the Tiefling, focusing on its rituals and practices. The Aurora believe in the inherent connectiveness of everything, and spend their lives understanding this.
 
**The Carnivale**  
The Carnivale are the lore keepers. Storytellers and Bards, the Carnivale believes in collecting the history of the world and telling its tales through spectacle.
 
## **Traits**

**Creature Type:** Humanoid 
**Size:** Medium (about 4-7 feet tall) or Small (about 3-4 feet tall), chosen when you select this species
**Speed:** 30 feet
As a Tiefling, you have the following special traits.  
**Darkvision.** You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.
**Tribal Influence.** You either grew up, or had parents who grew up in one of the Tiefling Tribes, and have inherited beliefs, practices, and rituals from them. Tieflings of course vary, as does their dedication to the practices and beliefs of their tribe, but those ties run deep. The most out of practice Aurora still knows the rituals, afterall.
When you reach character levels 3 and 5, you learn a higher-level spell, as shown on the table. You always have that spell prepared. You can cast it once without a spell slot, and you regain the ability to cast it in that way when you finish a Long Rest. You can also cast the spell using any spell slots you have of the appropriate level.
Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait (choose the ability when you select the legacy).
**Otherworldly Presence.** You know the Thaumaturgy cantrip. When you cast it with this trait, the spell uses the same spellcasting ability you use for your Fiendish Legacy trait
