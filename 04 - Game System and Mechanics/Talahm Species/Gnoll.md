**Natives of Southern Velustra, Gnolls have an unearned Reputation for savagry by the colonial powers. Gnolls instead represent a proud heritage of underserved people, their lands taken over desecrated, only to be given the reputation of backwards savages for daring to fight back. Gnolls are skeptical of the benefits of "progress" quick to point out that it's always built on the ones you're stepping over to get to.**
 
## **Traits**

**Creature Type:** Humanoid
**Size:** Medium (about 4-7 feet tall) or Small (about 3-4 feet tall), chosen when you select this species
**Speed:** 30 feet
**Darkvision.** You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.
**Self-Reliant.** Gnolls believe strongly in self-reliance. In that regard you gain proficiency in Survival and Perception
**Tenacity.** During a Short or Rest gain additional hitpoints equal to your level, if you are Maximum add the hitpoints from 1 rest to Temporary HP, does not stack.
**Defiant.** Advantage on saving throws vs._Frightened_ or _Charmed_ condition.
**Last Breath.** On death saving throws may still do a single action during throws, but will incur disadvantage on death saves.
