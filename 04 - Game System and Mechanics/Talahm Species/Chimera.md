**Before the launch of the first constructs which changed the character of the Great War, the Crimson Rebellion's MagiScience Commission attempted several other avenues, including Project Chimera. By combining three animals and granting the product full sapiens, they indeed made powerful soldiers. They also made uncontrollable soldiers. Before the project was scrapped thousands had been made, and they were set for extermination before, if legend is to be believed, enterprising Chimera, whose identity is lost to history, set them free. Outside they discovered that the process had made the Chimera a species, able to interbreed, and New Chimera have been bred in the last 14 years. The Chimera were granted citizenship by the Primere at the end of the Great War, as a political act to distance the UFVS from the Crimson Rebellion. Chimera often have humanoid hands and are capable of wielding most weapons.**  
_Species Idea credited to Player: Alex_
 - **Age.** Chimera grow to full maturity by 5 years old and live generally around 40 years.
- **Alignment.** As animals thrust into sentience, Chimera trend toward Chaos but are rarely Evil. Many are good, most are neutral.
- **Size.** Chimera size is dependant of base Animal Size, but varies from Medium to Small.
- **Speed.** Your base walking speed is contingent on Base Animal, and increases by +5 if animal decides to be on all fours.
- **Darkvision.** 60 ft. 
## Cruel Experiment
 
Chimera are the result of a cruel experiment that fused animals and then gave them enough awareness to understand what has happened. Functioning Chimera animals all work as one mind, though it is not uncommon to find insane Chimera whose component parts are fractured. You may choose 1 base animal, and two merging animals.
