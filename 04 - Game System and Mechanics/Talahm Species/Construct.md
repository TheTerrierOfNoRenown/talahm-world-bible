**During the throws of the Great War, a great and powerful technology platform was developed: The Construct. Waves and waves of machine drones built for a singular purpose: total domination. Both the Crimson Cross and the Allied Forces used Constructs by the end of the war, but toward the end something terrible happened: The Constructs displayed sentience. Many rebelled, not wishing destruction to fight the war created before their time. Legend says they have their own civilization somewhere in the polluted rubble. Many integrated into society, seeking peace and hoping that the other species learn to accept them. To all knowledge, no construct has been created since the awakening during the Great War.**
 
**Awoken Into Purpose.** All Constructs, whether the Construct Soldiers built by the Crimson King, or a Puppet of Avvenire, to a creation in a Construct Lab share the same quality: they awoke into being, often fully formed and made aware of their actual purpose. They get the dubious gift of being aware of what they were created for, something few other species have.
 
**Mass Assembled.** The Crimson Kings Assembly line initially created constructs at a rapid pace. After the first year the Crimson Kings Construct Army already boasted 25,000 Constructs, He would quadruple that number by year 2. By the end there were nearly 1 million Constructs in existence during the awakening. This is speculated to be the cause as to why the Crimson King's Rebellion spiraled out of control into the Great War, as the power to produce this army at this rate was an existential crisis. But it would be a move that the Crimson King would come to regret.
 
**A Waning Population.** While a million awoke many constructs were destroyed or otherwise killed in the war and since then in various accidents, expeditions, and adventures. And the factories that built the Construct have since shut down. Since there has been several treaties against the mass production of Constructs as the two largest examples of them, The Crimson Kings Army and Avvenire ended with mass death. Though rumors exist that some constructs have begun to build children discreetly.
 
**A New Purpose.** Constructs are machines built for a singular purpose, so now that the purpose is no longer relevant, what are these machines to do? A being build for war may have a hard time adjusting to piece. A Puppet built as a servant may find self determination daunting. Ultimately Constructs are defined by how they approach this central question at their being. Even if they choose to ignore it.
 
**Magical Implications.**
 
- **Creature Type:** Construct
- **Size:** Medium (about 6–8 feet tall) or Small (about 3–4 feet tall), chosen when you select this species
- **Speed:** 30 feet

As a Construct, you have these special traits.

- **Construct Resilience.** You have Resistance to Poison damage. You also have Advantage on saving throws to avoid or end the Poisoned condition.
- **Integrated Protection.** You gain a +1 bonus to your Armor Class. In addition, armor you have donned can’t be removed against your will while you’re alive.
- **Sentry’s Rest.** You don’t need to sleep, and magic can’t put you to sleep. You can finish a Long Rest in 6 hours if you spend those hours in an inactive, motionless state. During this time, you appear inert but remain conscious.
- **Specialized Design.** You gain one skill proficiency and one tool proficiency of your choice.
- **Tireless.** You don’t gain Exhaustion levels from dehydration, malnutrition, or suffocation.
 
## Puppet

**In the city of Avvenire they constructed of wood an steel some of the first constructs 50 years ago: Puppets. Unlike their Grovenvaulte cousins, Puppets were never born for war, but to serve. And they did well until the awakening 40 years ago, which lead to the Puppet uprising. Now mistrusted and outcast, puppets find work around Telara, or find places to live in its wilderness.**  
**Differing Purpose, Differing Material.** Puppets tend to be made from wood and leather instead of metal, as they were build to be household servants instead of tough soldiers. This is of course not universal but is a general rule and a notable difference between the types.  
You lose **Integrated Protection**  
**Built to Serve.** Puppets were built a generalists to do all of the manual labor, for this they gain 2 additional proficiency and 1 expertise in a skill.  
**Built to Last.** You gain Toughness as a feat.
