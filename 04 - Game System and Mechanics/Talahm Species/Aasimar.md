**Aasimar are not what they appear to be. Beings of pure light and magic, Aasimar first manifest in specific location where magic is powerful or wild, for this reason and their initial appearance, they are often associated with religious imagery. But an Aasimar cannot survive in this form alone for long, so they undergo a merging with a willing participant, and this merging blends their personalities. As a convenience they often take the name of the participant, and append a personal name on to it, Like Roddick-Tem. Aasimar have structures and groups called manifestations in every major city, where people make deals to become willing participants in the rituals, in exchange for favor.**   **Creature Type:** Humanoid  


**Size:** Depending on host  
**Speed:** Depending on host  

As an Aasimar, you have these special traits  

**Darkvision.** You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.  
**Magical Being.** As a magical being, Aasimar are tuned into magic, as such they have an innate knowledge of the weave, they gain one cantrip of their choice  
**Past Lives.** Aasimar inhabit several hosts in their life, and some of those traits have bled through, add one racial trait OR one level 1 non spellcasting class ability of your choice, the GM may choose to diminish its usefulness for balance.
**Host Ability.** Pick one racial trait from your hosts racial traits.
**Incorporeal Power.** When you reach character level 3, you can use an incorporial power as a Bonus Action using one of the options below (choose the option each time you transform). The transformation lasts for 1 minute or until you end it (no action required). Once you transform, you can’t do so again until you finish a Long Rest.
Once on each of your turns before the power ends, you can deal extra damage to one target when you deal damage to it with an attack or a spell. The extra damage equals your Proficiency Bonus, and the extra damage’s type is either Necrotic for Necrotic Shroud or Radiant for Heavenly Wings and Inner Radiance.

Here are the transformation options: 
**Heavenly Wings.** Two spectral wings sprout from your back temporarily. Until the transformation ends, you have a Fly Speed equal to your Speed. 
**Inner Radiance.** Searing light temporarily radiates from your eyes and mouth. For the duration, you shed Bright Light in a 10-foot radius and Dim Light for an additional 10 feet, and at the end of each of your turns, each creature within 10 feet of you takes Radiant damage equal to your Proficiency Bonus. 
**Necrotic Shroud.** Your eyes briefly become pools of darkness, and flightless wings sprout from your back temporarily. Creatures other than your allies within 10 feet of you must succeed on a Charisma saving throw (DC 8 plus your Charisma modifier and Proficiency Bonus) or have the Frightened condition until the end of your next turn.
