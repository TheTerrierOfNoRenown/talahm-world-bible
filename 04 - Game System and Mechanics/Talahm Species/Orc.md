**The Xuing Dynasty, among the oldest of civilizations in the Occident fell only recently the the Opal Revolution taking place in the month of Opal of 1712 ended it, and the Democratic Peoples Republic of Chuwing rose in its place. The Orcs, most associated with Chuwing were at first seen as allies by the UFVS, but now a cold war has emerged between the two, and Orcs, even ones with no ties to Chuwing are casted with suspicion.**
 
**Creature Type:** Humanoid 
**Size:** Medium (about 6-7 feet tall)
**Speed:** 30 feet
As an Orc, you have these special traits.
**Orcish Education.** Orcs from Chuwing attend some of the most regulated schools in the world, and have a reputation abroad for being good studies. You gain two additional proficiencies.
**Darkvision.** You have Darkvision with a range of 120 feet.
**Relentless Endurance.** Orcish discipline is revered around the world and can even pull them from the brink of death. When you are reduced to 0 Hit Points but not killed outright, you can drop to 1 Hit Point instead. Once you use this trait, you can't do so again until you finish a Long Rest.
