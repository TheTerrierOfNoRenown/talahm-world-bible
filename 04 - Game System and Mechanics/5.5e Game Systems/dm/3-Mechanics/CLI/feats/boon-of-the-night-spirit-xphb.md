# Boon of the Night Spirit
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 211. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*  

**Prerequisite**: 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Merge with Shadows.** While within [Dim Light](dim-light-xphb.md) or [Darkness](04%20-%20Game%20System%20and%20Mechanics/5.5e%20Game%20Systems/dm/3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/darkness-xphb.md), you can give yourself the [Invisible](conditions.md#Invisible) condition as a [Bonus Action](bonus-action-xphb.md). The condition ends on you immediately after you take an action, a [Bonus Action](bonus-action-xphb.md), or a [Reaction](reaction-xphb.md).

**Shadowy Form.** While within [Dim Light](dim-light-xphb.md) or [Darkness](04%20-%20Game%20System%20and%20Mechanics/5.5e%20Game%20Systems/dm/3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/darkness-xphb.md), you have [Resistance](04%20-%20Game%20System%20and%20Mechanics/5.5e%20Game%20Systems/dm/3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to all damage except Psychic and Radiant.
