Barton's Drug & Soda Jerk is a  corner-shop pharmacy located on the corner of Aberdeen Glas and Vaughan Spoke, Barton's in the premiere pharmacy in all of Briarbook. Founded in 1654 Barton's is a near 70 year old institution in the neighborhood. Originally an Apothecary, Barton specialized in tinctures, and since installing its Soda Fountain three years ago, Barton's now has become popular with neighborhood kids.

## Description

	Barton's Pharmacy has a long, wrap around windowed side, that looks clearly into a black and white checkered interior and a long white marble counter, and a brass spigot, the soda fountain. Behind the counter is a long display with several tonics, tinctures, and salves, along with mundane candy for the kids. Behind the counter is a Githyanki teenager, [[Eloba Gotsik]], who mans the Soda Jerk and handles the register.
