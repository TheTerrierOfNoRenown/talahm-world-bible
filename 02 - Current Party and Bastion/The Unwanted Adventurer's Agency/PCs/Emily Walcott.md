Emily Walcott is a Hedge Witch and magical expert for [[The Unwanted Adventurer's Agency]]. A self-taught diviner, Emily has proven herself to be a careful planner and fearless adversary. Her prodigal mastery of divining and almost prescient sense of forethought has become a necessary asset for UAA.

Emily moonlights as a magic tutor and Wizard-For-Hire for well off families, notably having trained [[Stella Thaumer]] during the early parts of her career. Partially due to her background as a street orphan Emily has a few contacts in the underworld, that gives her a slight edge against the more legit backgrounds of her peers.

Her magical abilities are described varied but efficient. Not one to use more movement than she has to to accomplish the spell, Emily is extremely efficient when she casts.

## Appearance 

Emily has dirty blonde hair that she wears in a messy bun. She wears a green duster, and has striking blue eyes. She is gaunt, as if food security is a new concept to her. Her eyes are strikingly blue, that pierce through you as she stares, and she wears a bored expression on her face when speaking to most people.

## Personality

Emily is efficient. Seeing little point in flourish, her work is done in the fewest component possible. Whereas others may make a show of a fire spell, Emily bought a flare gun. Emily is smart, quiet, and constantly observational, knowing enough to be to quick to speak. Emily has a keen sense of survival, this often translates to a 'flailing' in the battlefield, she she seeks safety and cover to do her spells. This panicky tendency, rather than leading to mistakes, is actually Emily's element.
 
## Associations

Emily is a member of the Unwanted Adventurer's Agency, and is part of the main crew with [[Franklin David 1]] and [[Battle Unit P-6K47 Pierce]]. She was the trainer for Stella Thaumer, and has outside association with a her  friend [[Isabel Bruno]], whom Emily regards as one of her first peers.

## Background

Emily was born 9 Topaz DL 1696, 7 years before the [[The Red Rebellion]]. She was largely raised by [[The Illuminated Church]] in the [[Fort Cape]] neighborhood of [[Baronsborough]] in [[Teylis-Mah'r]]. A precocious youth, when Emily wasn't getting into trouble with the law, she was doing odd jobs that would grant her access to libraries. Being poor, Emily could not afford an education beyond what was provided by the church, so she scrounged libraries and books stores and taught herself magic.

This hobby of hers made her a valuable asset to the various criminal families, to which she spent the bulk of the war assisting in magical ways in exchange for scrolls, libraries, and money. In DL 1710 Emily met Stella Thaumer after being hired as a tutor for her family. It was this experience that convinced Emily to leave crime behind and focus of her work as a tutor and a Wizard-For-Hire. 

Unfortunately that did not keep her out of trouble. Recently Emily had noticed a series of disappearances with people seemingly unaware of the disappearance or people are closed lipped about it. Having become nervous she had gotten too close, Emily fled her home in Fort Cape, soon meeting the UAA. Since Emily has become a valued member, but has never forgotten about the disappearances in Fort Cape.
