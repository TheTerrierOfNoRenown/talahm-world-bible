Franklin is the founder, owner, and leader of [[The Unwanted Adventurer's Agency]], along side his own side business: The Gilded Swingbox, a first of its kind Karaoke business operating out of Briarbrook. 

Franklin is an Entertainer by trade, but in many ways he views himself as a hype-man, breaking through the insecurities of his peers to show them their true potential. 

## Appearance

Franklin is a tall and wide orc who dressed in a silky dress shirt that can change color at will. He has a large Bushy beard, well groomed hared, and pronounced tusks that he brags are a sign of his virility. Franklin is often well-dressed, especially since the success of the UAA can allow him to afford it. 

## Personality

Franklin is a man that is hard not to like. Charismatic and Understanding of hardship, Franklin sees the best in people, and it is this talent that has made him such a good talent scout, not just for running his Karoake business but for the UAA. Franklin is described as easy-going by his peers.
