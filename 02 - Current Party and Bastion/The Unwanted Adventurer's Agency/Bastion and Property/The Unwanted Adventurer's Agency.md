Founded in Opal of 1721, following the attack on the Festival of Broken Masks by [[Pellnast Sailborn Coinminter|Crimson King]] Loyalist [[Coalpour Doortak Gearoiler]], where a small group of adventurer's lead by found [[Franklin David]] saved [[Mayor Harrowsfeld]], the group was approached by a corporate warlock: [[Isaiah Wayfinder]] representing the talent agency *(Abyssal name that to your ear sounds like a cacophony of screams and pleads for help that grow increasingly desperate then subsiding to a whimpering)* or in Common tongue: [[Crowhart & Orthax]]. 

Since its founding the UAA has billed itself as Generalist Adventurers Agency, but so far have largely done local neighborhood gigs, such as taking down a Luck Demon and his Magical Casino and fighting a Dragon on top of [[Anchorwench Tower]]. They are hoping to make a name for themselves as a premiere [[2035C Adventurer's Agency|Adventurer's Agency]].

## Description

The Unwanted Adventurer's Agency is located in [[Briarbrook]] at the corner of Marigold and Josaphina Ave. The agency itself is a corner shop, with a windowed facade and a green awning leading to a green wooden door with a Bronze Plaque saying "The Unwanted Adventurer's Agency."

 Inside is a hardwood floor, wood panel wall front office, containing a desk where receptionist [[Gladys Belladonna]] is stationed. Inside the windowed walls are lined with green cushioned chairs flanked on both sides by potted plants.
