The beloved receptionist for [[The Unwanted Adventurer's Agency]]. Gladys is a cheerful gal with a studious eye for detail and a mind that can contain the reference for an entire filing system. Gladys is the core of the agency, answering the phones, taking care of the filing, billing, bank work, and managing the Calendar. She is also the chief laison to [[Isaiah Wayfinder]] and [[Crowhart & Orthax]], both whom she worked for before her job at the agency.

Gladys is good, but she is not a hero, and has her foibles. Such as completely forgetting about [[Deter Longtooth]]. whom she was worried about, when given a 300 Stick Bonus by [[Franklin David]], so that she could buy new shoes.

## Appearance

Gladys is a 7' tall green [[Dragonborn]] with a purple underbelly and teal horns. Gladys frequently wears several plain bracelets and rings, and [[neck plates]] under her ears that are a form of Dragonborn jewelry. She has a thin locket, whose contents she carefully guards. While Gladys is typically very fashionable, she often prefers sun dresses and a stylish hat, Yellow being her favorite.

Gladys has a raspy urbane voice.

## Personality 

Gladys is the finest receptionist in all of [[Baronsborough]] and probably all of [[Teylis-Mah'r]] and she knows it. This leads to an air of deserved self-confidence that comes from the peak of her trade. She is dutiful and diligent, and she of course can be bribed. 

Gladys is not greedy but has a practical relationship with money. Money buys her things that she like, and she likes what she likes. 

Gladys is above all loyal, though she can be bribed by people who she likes and respects, she cannot be bribed to betray them

## Associations

Gladys, being an employee of [[The Unwanted Adventurer's Agency]] works with [[Franklin David]], [[Battle Unit P-6K47 Pierce]], and [[Emily Walcott]]. She is also friendly with her former boss: [[Isaiah Wayfinder]]. 

Gladys has a friendship with [[Deter Longtooth]], whom she likes, but not-in-that-way. 

## Background

Gladys is the former Secretary to [[Isaiah Wayfinder]].
