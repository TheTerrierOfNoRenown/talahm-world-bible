Deter Longtooth is the longtime ally of [[The Unwanted Adventurer's Agency]], and in many ways is their most ardent believer. A consumate, if perpetually unlucky friend, Deter is always willing to give a hand to the Agency when they need an extra one. He has an unrequited love of UAA receptionist [[Gladys Belladonna]].  

## Appearance

Deter is a tall, thin Gnoll, with mangy brownish fur and totally brown eyes. Deter is generally seen wearing an ill fitting white button up, tweed pants, an untailored tweed vest, and a pair of scuffed dress shoes that look uncannily like bowling shoes. Presumably at some point he had a coat but nobody has seen him with it. People suspect this is the only suit he owns. He stands almost with a perpetual slouch, which he only straightens up with great focus.

## Personality 

Deter is most often nervous, even as he is quite brave. Deter is not a hero, and has not aspirations to be. But he respects the members of the UAA because of their strength and willingness to stand up for little guys. Deter is ambitious, wanting more than anything to be a business owner, but he suffers from a lack of directions, which often leads him to execute on poorly thought out plans, such as taking out a mob loan of 50,000  to buy a lot of paperclips in order to start a Paperclip business.

Overall, Deter is a well meaning, but doesn't often think things through.

## Associations

Deter is the owner and sole proprietor of [[Longtooth Office Supplies]]. He is a frequent patron of [[The Easy Speak]], and former unfortunate victim of [[The Last Dice]]. Deter had a deal with [[Anchorwench Tower]], before its Chief Executive, and Warlock [[Radley Brickerfall]] met his untimely demise at the hands of the Indigo Dragon [[Bellow]]. Deter is also associated with the [[Rosa Family]], particularly [[Capo Yogth]], with whom he originally made the deal for the paperclip money. 

Deter is of course also associated with [[The Unwanted Adventurer's Agency]], having met [[Franklin David]], [[Battle Unit P-6K47 Pierce]], and [[Sinn Firedusk]] during his trouble with [[The Last Dice]] Casino. Deter has an unrequited attraction to [[Gladys Belladonna]]

## Background

Deter Longtooth was born 12 Ruby, DL 1693 in Briarbrook to [[Ida Longtooth]]. Deter never knew his father. Deter grew up in the neighborhood of Briarbrook, scraping together enough money to attend [[Leaf-Gauge School of Business, Finance, and Food Delivery]], at 24 Deter had earned a Bachelors in Marketing and 30 Minute Deliveries. Since then Deter has attempted to establish [[Longtooth Office Supplies]], to which he hopes to one day turn into an actual business, but for now only is able to sell paperclips to a handful of businesses.

One 20 Sapphire, 1721, Deter, following a seemingly successful business meeting with [[Anchorwench Tower]], had decided to step into [[The Last Dice]] Casino in order to celebrate, unknowingly stepping into the realm of a Luck Demon, and sapping away their Luck. He then contacted [[The Unwanted Adventurer's Agency]], causing a quite a scene as his bad luck accidentally tore apart their office. He was helped by [[Franklin David]], [[Sinn Firedusk]], and [[Battle Unit P-6K47 Pierce]], getting his luck restored following the destruction of the Luck Demon.

Since, Deter has spent a lot of his offtime around the Agency, assisting [[Gladys Belladonna]], to whom he harbors a crush. Sometime in the intervening, Deter contact [[Capo Yogth]], a member of the [[Rosa Family]], for a loan of 5,000 Sticks or 50,000 [[Currency|Rubradium]]. He has since defaulted on the loan, and has been taken to the Underdark in order to exact a price.
