Stella Thaumer is a Sorceress, Adjunct Professor at [[Telenast University]], and a former pupil of [[Emily Walcott]]. She moonlights as a member of [[The Unwanted Adventurer's Agency]], though she has been far too busy at work lately to assist. Stella is a powerful Sorceress, and consummate networker, who is good at making and maintaining connections everywhere.

## Appearence

Stella Thaumer is tall, black, and beautiful, with long and tall black hair.
