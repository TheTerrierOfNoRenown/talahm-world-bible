Located on the corner of Aberdeen Glas and Haywood Spur, this magical emporium is owned and operated by the Magister and Simulacrum duo: [[Brumig Brasscompass|Brumig & Brumig]]. The Broom Closet is the best Magic Shop in all of [[Baronsborough]], boasting some of the finest magical items, toys, and knicknacks, this side of Elhana's Canal.

The Broom Closet is a Magic Shop with an array of magic odds ends and oddities. Rarely keeping a magic weapon, but generally keeping something useful.

## Description

A small street shop, with wood paneling and purple awnings outside, and a large display window on the right side of the green door, displaying a floating and slowly rotating gyroscope, the door has a large glass panel on the front with the words "The Broom Close" and "Est. 1708", in radial text forming a circle around a decal of a flying broom. 

Inside is a small cozy shops with a few tables, some shelves on the right wall, and a long wooden counter with a display, and a door behind the counter going into the back rooms. Behind the counter will always be one of the Brumigs with the other usual in the back.
