The rough but wry owner and bartender at [[The Broken Lantern]] tavern located in the [[The Purple Arches District]] neighborhood of [[Unterveil]]. Brody is considered a pillar of the community, often serving as a bridge for people who fall between the cracks and necessary services that are less than legal. Brody is people first, and rules far second. He is best friends with [[Brayson]] and [[Shaver Cricks]], to whom he has a long and storied past. He is as well the adopted father of [[Bailey Sloicht]] and [[Gretchen Sloicht]].

Brody has a past, and given his deep connections with associates with the [[Rosa Family]], it's likely said past was less than legal. Among those connections is a strained relationship with crooked cop [[Renard Manacle]], to whom Brody holds in contempt. Likewise he acts as a reluctant informant for the movement of the [[Rosa Family]] for [[Chief Steele]]. 

## Appearence

Brody stands at a proud 4'7", which is quite tall for a Duergar. He is bald with light blue skin and glimmering pale-silver eyes. He has a bushy white beard. He is often seen in a plaid button-up and a smock, and stands on a stool behind the raised bar so he doesn't seem so short when people walk in.

## Personality

Brody is wry with an ornery streak. Described as hard-headed, Brody has always had a hard time not listening to his heart. He has a strong sense of right and wrong, a sense he says he ignored too long in his life and refuses to ignore further. Brody cares more about people than nonsense about "order" or "justice", and uses his position as a community and bartender for the forgotten to help people. Whether it's setting them up with a guide for the Underdark or getting people connected with cheap medicines and potions for their aches and pains, Brody is always there to help.

Brody is however, a hothead, and prone to shoot from the hip, which often gets him in trouble, and he usually needs [[Brayson]] or [[Shaver]] to bail him out. 

## Associations

Brody is possibly the most well connected person in the entirety of [[Unterveil]]. He has connections with the police through [[Chief Steele]], [[The Rosa Family]] through his own past, he acts as a primary bridge to [[Brayson]] to attain a guide for travelers in the [[Underdark]], and of course he has a few connections with the [[Myconid]], particularly [[Lichen]], the communicator for the Myconid in Unterveil. 

## Background

 Brody believes he was born in the Summer of 1633 making him 89 years old. He has only had a birthday since 1720 when his adopted daughters [[Bailey Sloicht]] and [[Gretchen Sloicht]] assigned him the Birthday of 31 Garnet. 

Brody was born to Siobhán Sloicht, a house servant to [[The Rosa Family]], and grew up as playmates to Xander Boshkt, the father of [[Ren Boshkt]], the current Don of the Rosa Family. This connection gave him first job as a muscle for The Rosa Family at 16 years old. He became a made man by 22, and one of the most respected advisers and fixers until his unexpected retirement at 86. 

While it is well known this move upset Ren, it was allowed because of his 70 years of loyal service, and he still occasionally advised Ren, and views him as a nephew.

It was during this service that he met [[Shaver Cricks]] and [[Brayson]], as they were invaluable to navigating the [[Underdark]] and the sewers, which was the primary mode in which Brody did his movement in the surface world. He has known the two for decades, having met Shaver as a child, and Brayson while traveling the Underdark road two decades ago.

Three years ago Brody unexpectedly quit after a messy job that he was fixing for, and suddenly adopted two young girls, Bailey and Gretchen, who he raises as his own. He has seemingly gone legit, not actively engaging in crime, this has come with compromises. Brody informs dangerous secrets about the movements of the Rosa Family to Chief Steele as a means to keep the [[Unterveil Police Department]] from cracking down on the people of [[The Purple Arches District]].
