Gretchen Sloicht is the younger Sloicht sister. Gretchen is the bald and sickly counterpart to her older sister [[Bailey Sloicht]].  Gretchen is a hedge witch, and very talented for her age.

## Appearance

Gretchen is bald and sickly looking, though she does not actually have a chronic illness. Bailey suggests this was immaciation as a result of the troubles with their birth father. Gretchen is often seen wearing a dark hooded sweatshirt and jeans.

## Personality 

Gretchen is timid and full of doubt. Though brilliant by means of magic, she has difficulty with social or situational intelligence. She relies heavily on her older sister [[Bailey Sloicht]], but often has strained relationships with everyone else.


## Associations

Gretchen is the junior member of the [[Purple Arches Crew]], so is closely associated with [[Denise Jayson]] and [[Brandon 'Hopper' Bricks]], she is as well the  younger sister of [[Bailey Sloicht]], whom she is worried she is a disappointment to.

Gretchen is the adoptive daughter of [[Brody Sloicht]], and is well associated with [[The Broken Lantern]], she is the goddaughter of [[Brayson]].

## Background

Gretchen Sloicht was born 19 Peridot, DL 1711, under the name Gretchen Violette [[de la Rosé von Fleur]], to [[Hortense Pivoine del la Rosé von Fleur]] and [[Anémone Zinnia de la Rosé von Fleur]].  Her mother died at her birth. She has no memories of her, only memories of Bailey, and their abusive father. Gretchen does not talk about those times.
