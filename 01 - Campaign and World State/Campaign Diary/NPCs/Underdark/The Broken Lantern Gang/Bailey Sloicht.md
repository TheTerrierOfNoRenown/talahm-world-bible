Bailey Sloicht is the Eldest Sloicht sister, together with [[Gretchen Sloicht]], they are the adoptive daughters of [[Brody Sloicht]]. They are the informal leaders of the small street gang: [[Purple Arches Crew]]. Bailey Sloicht, the older Sloicht sister is looked up to by the rest of the crew as being the leader and chief plotter of the gang 

## Appearance

Bailey is sleight framed and almost always in a white hooded shirt and running shoes, carrying a baseball bat wrapped in barbed wire. She is obviously very young, and carries herself with a chip on her shoulder, as if she has something to prove.

## Personality 

Bailey is a brash plotter, always looking for the new score. Terrified of losing control of her situaton again, Bailey wants to ensure that her family is never put under the thumb of a cruelty by working for the one form of power she knows: money. 

Bailey is highly protective of her crew, willing to go any distance for any one of them.

## Associations

Bailey is the de facto leader of the [[Purple Arches Crew]], so is closely associated with [[Denise Jayson]] and [[Brandon 'Hopper' Bricks]], she is as well the older sister of [[Gretchen Sloicht]], and is particularly protective over her.

Bailey is the adoptive daughter of [[Brody Sloicht]], and is well associated with [[The Broken Lantern]], she is the goddaughter of [[Brayson]].

## Background

Bailey Sloicht was born 09 Garnet, DL 1708, under the name Bailey Chartreuse [[de la Rosé von Fleur]], to [[Hortense Pivoine del la Rosé von Fleur]] and [[Anémone Zinnia de la Rosé von Fleur]].  Her mother died in early childhood and Bailey has very few memories of her. What she does have memories of is the monster her father became.

Her father, grief struck and resentful had turned monsterously abusive toward his daughters. Bailey doesn't like to recall these times, as she bared the brunt of the abuse, being the oldest and most protective. 

It was for this reason that the assassination of her father by [[Brody Sloicht]] came as such a welcome surprise. That night, five years ago, she followed a shady assassin into [[Unterveil]] from her [[Glennave]] high rise, because she was certain that whatever life he has was better than the one she came to.

After a long time under his protection she came to view Brody as the real father she never had, and has decided to follow in his footsteps, not as a barkeep, but as the criminal who saved her life.
