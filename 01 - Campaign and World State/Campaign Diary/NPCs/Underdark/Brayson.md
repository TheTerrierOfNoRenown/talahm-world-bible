#Underdark #Unterveil #Sloicht 
Brayson is [[Quaggoth]] and an Underdark Traveling guide for [[Lester & Rot]]. A long time associate of [[Brody Sloicht]], Brayson has worked odd jobs with Brody and [[Shaver Cricks]] for years. Brayson briefly worked for [[The Rosa Family]], though to hear him tell it he really worked for Brody.

Brayson is the calm, world weary anchor to Shaver and Brody's edge, often the voice of caution and reason for the group. This is a stark contrast to who Brayson was when they first met. Brayson takes specific joy in helping others, though this does not translate to others of his own kind, whom he still regards with suspicion. 

## Appearence

Brayson is 10' tall, towering over most people, and is said to resemble a Grizzly Bear in a duster. Despite his origins, Brayson is well kept, having regular appointments at [[Cuan's Barber Shop]]. Brayson's wears a long Wool Jacket and a Flannel Shirt, with thick baggy breaches specially designed for him. Brayson is always barefoot (or bear foot, as he jokes), as he feels it's necessary to navigate the Underdark with all senses. 

## Personality

Brayson is easy to get along with. Patient and funny, Brayson is prone to puns and well equipped with a story. Brayson, despite his nature, is the calmest of his group, having learned from them that patience often gives better results than rushing headlong in. Brayson is kind and empathetic as well as prudent and careful. Brayson is slow to act, and often slower to judge. 

Brayson is weary of the danger of his work, and his slow to act tendency gives way to an intense instinct for safety while he is in the tunnels of the Underdark. 

## Assosiations

Brayson is the best friend of [[Brody Sloicht]] and [[Shaver Cricks]], as well as the godfather to [[Bailey Sloicht]] and [[Gretchen Sloicht]]. Having known Brody and Shaver for 24 years, Brayson trusts them greater than he felt it was possible to trust anyone. Brayson would go through the Abyss and the Nine Hells for the Sloichts and Cricks, and they know they wouldn't have to ask.

Brayson has also been associated with [[Lester & Rot]] as an employee for 10 years, making a fine living at 10 Sticks a day plus commission. Despite this, Brayson lives above [[The Broken Lantern]], as a way to keep an eye on the Sloichts when he is in town. 

Brayson also has a friendly relationship to [[Cuan the Barber]], as he has been going to his Barbershop for over 10 years. 

## Backstory

Brayson believes he was born somewhere between DL 1682 and  DL 1689, usually reporting he is about 36 years old. This is canonized when his godchildren gave him the birthday of 19 Emerald, DL 1686, after finding out that as [[Quaggoth]] who lived in the [[Underdark]] his entire life, he had no idea exactly when he was born. 

As a Quaggoth, Brayson was near solitary his entire life, only learning to speak through several sets of associations with criminals who would regularly keep him as an attack dog while in the Underdark. For the first approximately 16 years of his life, Brayson mostly knew social interaction as territorial disputes and friendships of convenience. 

It was because of this that Brayson was wholly unequipped for when [[Brody Sloicht]] saved him from an Illithid prison when he was 12 years old. Brayson had no idea how to handle this situation, and held deep suspicions as to why Brody broke him out, going so far as to vow to pay back Brody Sloicht, by saving his. 

Brody, amused by the arrangement, humored the young Brayson as they traveled the Underdark, but try as he might, Brayson could not repay the debt, largely because of Brody's competence and cautiousness, and partly because of his friendship with the old, alert, and slightly crazy [[Shaver Cricks]].

It took 5 years for the opportunity to pay back the debt presented itself and Brayson took it. But by then he had no desire to return to a life of solitude, as having someone he could trust like Brayson and even Shaver was far more valuable. Brayson would continue to work with Brody for nearly two decades, before leaving crime altogether to join [[Lester & Rot]] as a tunnel guide for the underdark. Brayson still spend all of his off time around Brody.

He is trusted so much, that Brody made Brayson the godfather of his adopted daughters: [[Bailey Sloicht]] and [[Gretchen Sloicht]]. A role Brayson takes incredibly seriously.
