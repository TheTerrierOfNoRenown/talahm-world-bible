Rubies is the state sponsored currency of the UFVS, made completely of Rubradium, a red tinted metal, warm to the touch used in jewelry and some magic applications, or of Conchillium, a purple metal used in jewelry.

The smallest unit, a set of 1 cm x 1 cm cube of this with a stamp of a “1” on one side called the “face”, called a ‘square’ or colloquially a ‘pebble’. The most common unit, a "Stick" is a 1 cm x 1 cm x 10 cm bar of Rubradium worth 10 pebbles. 

Because of shortages and weight concerns, it has become more common to keep bankers notes on your person instead of actual Rubies, which are generally stored in Fort Haze in those forms. Pebbles are still in use to break Ruby Bank notes, which have adopted the "Sticks" Terminology. Though aside from colloquialisms the other denominations are rarely used. "Sticks and Pebbles" are thus used to refer to all currency.

"You're Stick and Pebbling" is a common phrase for creating a huge deficit through small denominations, as in "nickel and diming" in out dimensions with less fun monetary histories.
