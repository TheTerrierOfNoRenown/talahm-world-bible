## Recap

*Last time*

[[The Unwanted Adventurer's Agency]], having come back from a job involving a Wizard's Tower received news from local paperboy: [[Connor Trak]]. [[Deter Longtooth]], friend of the UAA and local paperclip salesman, had gone missing. 

Opting out of searching his apartment, the group instead went directly to the local [[Pumphouse]], which served as the main entrance into [[Unterveil]]. Unfortunately on the way they ran into the [[Baron Dernier]] as well as [[Pete & Re-Pete]] back from the dead. After a tense and near-death fight, the crew reflected on their near-loss as they descended into Unterveil.

Upon reach Unterveil the crew met [[Daefi Glimmersky]], as well as [[Renoir and Marie]], as they were trying to get information on Unterveil, promising Marie that they would take her and Renoir to Briarbrook when they were done, they joined up with Daefi who said he knew a place where they could get info. They also met [[Awakened Unit M-29G6 Stitch]], who was caring after the a set of [[Construct|Constructs]] who have stopped moving, stopped interacting, and succumbed to a condition known as [[The Waiting]]. He was announcing the grand opening of [[The First Church of the Clockwork God]]. The players decided to follow Daefi to [[The Broken Lantern]]. But before they could make their way an explosion occurred near the Pumphouse elevator, where they saw two young girls, one in which could cast sigil magic, fled. After a brief and intense exchange between Daefi and [[Renard Manacle]], which nearly ended with [[Franklin David]] punching Renard, they opted out of chasing the girls to contain the fire. [[Battle Unit P-6K47 Pierce]], put out the fire with a well placed Ice Grenade, and they met [[Radley Steele|Chief Steel]] and [[Strategy Unit R-86T12 Hunch]] in the fire, where they discussed what happened.

We left off as the crew were making their way to [[The Broken Tavern]].

## Opening Monologue

Time marches forward. Even in fantasy, a civilization can only stay still for long, eventually, by inches or by leaps it must progress. Mysticism makes way for science, tools evolve into technology. And on Talahm, they made of magic what some worlds made of physics. Cities grew, the population became enlightened, and the new Golden Age of Talahm began. But progress comes at a cost. Capital, Inequality, Wars. 18 years ago, a fascist leader by the name of [[Pellnast Sailborn Coinminter|The Crimson King]], ran a march up [[The United Federation of Vassal States]], and it was only through blood, sweat, and luck that they stopped the march and ended his reign. No matter what we want, what we dream of, we must first heal, not just the scars on the land, but the scars on our hearts that pull us apart and force us to not trust each other. For that we must take the Long Road. 

## Set the Scene

The air is unsettlingly clean, stale in a way in which a normal city should not be. You all walk along the street, following a red skinned Tiefling named [[Daefi Glimmersky|Daefi]], down an old style cobblestone road. Surrounding you are short, but stacked buildings that look less construct, but grown, festered out of the ground, displacing the cobblestone to assert themselves. Despite their grown nature, and the mushroom carapaces that act like awnings and roofs, they resemble commercial buildings from the top side. Windowed galleries, discrete business doors, doors surrounded by bay windows, even some stairs that go down underground to reach a basement business. A common feature of the building around you are stairs leading up to the roof, as the roof often contains another building, grown from the roof, and so on and so forth. All the way above you, so distant you sometimes forget its there is the cavern roof above, with stalactites drooping down toward the ground. 

Daefi leads the way.

## Encounter A - Crossing the Rot House

Journal Picture - [[Outside the Rot House.jpg]] 

Daefi stops the party, noting that "There must be a breach around the rot house" and suggests that they hold their breath walking through this next part.

The party needs to make a constitution saving throw (DC - 10) or they  begin to wretch, could even throw up. 

## Encounter B - Tense Encounter at the Broken Lantern

Foundry Scene - The Broken Lantern
Journal Picture - [[The Broken Lantern.png]]
Key Characters - [[Brody Sloicht]], [[Brayson]], [[Shaver Cricks]], [[Renard Manacle]]. 

Situation - Characters walk into a conversation between Brody and Renard. 

Renard Goal: Wants to gain information on Brody's daughters, or goad him into going out and looking for them so Renard cal follow.
Brody Goal: Get Renard out of the bar so he can look for his daughters and ensure their safety. 

Situation - Brody needs the players to find his daughters, in return he will convince Brayson and Cricks to lead them to the place he thinks Deter is held.

#### Outside the Tavern

After the encounter at the Tavern, the players will encounter [[Strategy Unit R-86T12 Hunch]], he will offer assistance, either telling them where the girls are, or explaining how Renard is casing the neighborhood to give them advantage in avoiding Renard. He is cagey about his motivations, but he believes that the girls are a dead end, and it is best for him that the girls are not captured.

## Encounter C - A Meeting at the Abandoned Theater

Foundry Scene - Theatre
Key Characters: [[Bailey Sloicht]], [[Gretchen Sloicht]], [[Denise Jayson]], [[Brandon 'Hopper' Bricks]]. 

Situation - The [[Purple Arches Crew]] is held up inside the [[Crysalith Theater]]. The crew needs to figure out a way to approach the girls in a way that doesn't frighten them. The characters also must get to the girls without leaving a trace for [[Renard Manacle]] to follow. This can trigger the Renard Manacle fight if careless. If the fight happens here, Manacle will intentionally target the Sloicht sisters.

## Encounter D - Back at the Lantern

If the players successfully retrieves Bailey and Gretchen, they can bring them back to the Broken Lantern, they will go in the back door and encounter a brief encounter between Brody and [[Radley Steele|Chief Steele]].  

After the scene Brody will inform the crew that he needs to find a place to store the girls while the heat dies down.
