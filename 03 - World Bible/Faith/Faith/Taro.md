Tiefling believe in a form of animism, where the world exists in a connected web, and all things are united in this web called Taro. Taro is the name of the web, as well as the name of the spirits that inhabit all things, people, places, circumstances, and events. Everything has Taro, as all things are made from Taro, and while few are named the vast majority just exist, guiding the thing to its fated conclusion.
