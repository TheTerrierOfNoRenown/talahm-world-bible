Vigil is the name of the weekly and biweekly church days, where congregants go on Soldays and one other day depending on the specific church, to listen to the Radiant Word by a Beacon or a Bearer. The standard Vigil is broken up as following:

1. Greetings of Light
2. Study of a passage or a chapter of scripture
3. Discussion on life lessons from the passage
4. A reaffirmation of faith called "The Basking"
5. A recognition of the light in all called "The Offer of Peace"
6. A ceremony where the congregate walk by a Brazier at the alter and pass their hand through it called "The Sacrament of the Light."
7. Final words and administration
8. Final Prayer.
 
These are all broken up by standard prayers and songs, and a complicated order of sitting, standing, and kneeling. That standard Vigil is difficult for non Lumins to follow.
