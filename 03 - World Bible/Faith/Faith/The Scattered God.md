In the beginning there was light. Pure white Light, containing all but having no difference, no variance. And then it scattered creating color and substance and life. And thus was the God Scattered into its glorious radiance, and things came of it.
 
The Scattered Light is the deity of the Illuminated Church, The Orthodoxy of the Scattered Light, The Prismatic Churches, and the offshoot Nimos Faith.  
The Scattered Light in most interpretations is depicted as monotheistic deity with seven distinct forms:

1. The Radiance of the Harvest Moon - Red -Associated with Life, War, and the Harvest. Domains: Life, War, Nature
2. The Warmth of Ember Flame - Orange - Associated with Civilization, Forging, and the Hearth. Domains: Forge, Order, Knowledge
3. The Brilliance of the Shining Sun - Yellow - Associated with Nature, Peace, and Property. Domains: Nature, Peace, Order
4. The Bright of the Summer's Day – Green – Associated with Health, Peace, and Magic. Domains: Life, Peace, Arcana
5. The Gleaming of the Shifting Skies – Blue – Associated with Weather, Magic, and Unpredictability. Domains: Tempest, Trickery, Arcana
6. The Glow of Dusk – Indigo – Associated with Death and the coming end Domains: Grave, Death, Twilight
7. The Luster of Magic Shine – Associated with Wizardry, the Weave, and the Fae Domain: Knowledge, Twilight, Arcana
