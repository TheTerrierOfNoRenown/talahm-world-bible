Are Miracle Workers blessed by their various Gods or are they just specialized sorcerers? This is the subject of entire books of debate within the magical community. To be fair there are precedent to both.
 
Tyloq Wyrmwood of Uqerine University in Uhterhest argues that perhaps Miracle Workers are a specialized form of Warlock, who encounter powerful celestial beings and make pacts with them.
 
Ahnknasht the Unconquerable of Telenast University states that perhaps Miracle Work is more of a recessive trait of sorcery that can be unlocked through times of stress, citing the not uncommon tendency for sorcerers to cast divine magic.
