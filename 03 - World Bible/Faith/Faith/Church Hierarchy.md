1. Prime Lens - Leader of the Church, Chief Kanoun.
2. Lens - A Counsel of 200 or so Kanoun who select the Prime Lens
3. Kanoun - A high ranking member who overseas several churches, does not have to be a Bearer. Hon.: Grandfather, Grandmother, Grandparent.
4. Bearer - A miracle worker for the church. Priests and Priestesses who do the main work. Hon: Father, Mother, or Parent.
5. Beacon - A Preacher for a single church or chapel.
6. Tutor - A person who gives their life to the church but shares no special rank.
    1. Tutor can be sequestered into mountains for special projects that connect them to the light, like making an ancient herb liquor or making copies of the Radiant Word.
    2. Tutors can be in local convents and assist with Beacon or Bearer duties, or in education.
    3. Tutors can be martial masters, trained to protect Bearers and the interests of the church.
