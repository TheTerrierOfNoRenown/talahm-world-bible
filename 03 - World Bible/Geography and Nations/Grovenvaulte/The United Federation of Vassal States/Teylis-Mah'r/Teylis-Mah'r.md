#city
Teylis-Mah'r, the twin city united is the Capital City of the United Federation of Vassal States, a newish state built 98 years ago after the Vassal Rebellion, which split seven colonies from the Uhterhest Empire. Teylis was founded 250 years ago by Uhterhest settlers, but the ancient city of Mah'r stretches back time immemorial, and itself has a long history. Mah'r has seen and played part in dozens of the rise and falls of empires, and its ancient history of resilience is a point of pride of the Mah'rish citizens.
 
Teylis-Mah'r holds the seat of government of the UFVS, the Shimmering Spire that holds the Premier, the Parliament of Alderman, the Peoples Council, and the High Court stands in the center and at the top of Mah'r, an island off of the coast of Teylis, which was once an Island mountain that through terraforming and design has become the carved city that it is.
 
Teylis-Mah'r is ruled over by the Twin Mayors, who are elected every 6 years one by the Peninsula of Teylis, and the other by the proud island of Mah'r, and is legislated by the counsel of alderman, and the popular senate.
 
Once a year Mah'r celebrates the death of the Fire Tyrant in the Festival of Broken Masks.
