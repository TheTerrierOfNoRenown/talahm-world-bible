#crime
The Three families: the Rosa, the Copecelli, and the Tsikamori are the three main crime families in Teylis-Mah'r
 
Rose - Finacial Crime, mostly based in Unterveil. Its head, the notoriously private Don Boshkt, lives in an unknown location that must be teleported to. Capo G'eldaghar handles the day to day.
 
Copecelli - Drugs, based in the middle of Baronsborough, the Copecelli family runs the most sophisticated drug outfit in the city.
 
Tsikamori - Muscle, the first foreign based gang, mostly populated with Baohu Orcs, they provide muscle to anyone willing to hire.
