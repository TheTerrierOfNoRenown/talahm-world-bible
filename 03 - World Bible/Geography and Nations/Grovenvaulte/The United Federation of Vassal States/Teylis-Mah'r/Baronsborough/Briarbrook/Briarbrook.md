Briarbrook is the center neighborhood in [[Baronsborough]], characterized largely by the circularly manmade river, [[Elhana’s Canal]], that acts as  a moat around the neighborhood. This harkens back to its origins, where it was the location of [[Burg Fritz]], a castle built by the [[Fritz Clan]] in DL 1412 and still occupies the center of Briarbrook, albeit now as a Museum.
 
Now Briarbrook is a middle-lower end housing and commercial district, given its proximity to the docks and the Industrial District. It holds the dubious honor of being the most upper class neighborhood in all of Baronsborough, as well as the entrance to [[Unterveil]], at the pumphouse

## Appearence

Briarbrook is a circular neighborhood, surrounded by Elhana's Canal, a circular river that is around a block wide. The museum, Burg Fritz, sits at the center. Briarbrook is mainly comprised of office buildings, low rises, and a few historic homes. With only a single tower [[Raven's Perch]], and a few high rises such as [[Anchorwench Tower]], to break up the low rises. Briarbrook does have newer slated roads for the new [[Automotive]] industry.

## Infrastructure and Economy

The majority of work in Briarbrook is service and office work, as it acts as the hub for trade in all of [[Baronsborough]]. Often derisively referred to as the Poor's [[Glennave]], Briarbrook represents the central hub for the rest of the industrial district. An office area for the docks and factories south and east. 

Briarbrook has been recently modified to handle Automotive traffic, so out of neighborhood commutes have become easier. Along with this Briarbrook holds the honor of being the location of the primary [[Pumphouse]] for [[Teylis-Mah'r]], and the entrance into [[Unterveil]]. Because of this, despite being a hub for business in Baronsborough, the economy of Briarbrook is quite weak, though being part of the largest city in all of [[The United Federation of Vassal States]], keeps its economy stronger than most.

## Politics

Briarbrook is part of the larger [[Teylis-Mah'r]] so is not only part of the UFVS Republic state in [[Mah'r]], but also part of the local political infrastructure. Being looked after by the Mayor of Teylis [[Victor Ng]], but also represented by [[Walter Verdigris]] on the [[Popular Senate]], and Alderman [[Fildor Strigsamp Quickstitch]] in the [[Counsel of Aldermen]].
