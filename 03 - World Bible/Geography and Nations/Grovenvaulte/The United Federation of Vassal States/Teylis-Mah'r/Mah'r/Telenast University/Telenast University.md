Among the most presitigious Magical Universities in all of Grovenvaulte, Telenast was founded in [[Mah'r]] in the year 1156 by Ezekial Isaac Telenast, the Wizard of the legendary Heroes Group: The Broken Mask. Sitting atop the cloud tops on Ancient Mah'r, Telenast University resembles an old palace with floating turrets and towers connected by flow metal bridges which are warded from the elements with powerful magic. The grounds of Telenast are well kept by **Nobnail Rowshine Wickerweaver**, an extremely old Witch and Groundskeepers, and her army of unseen servants.
 
**The Staff**
 
The Dean of Telenast, **Archwizard Pyrite Lightscatter** is a Crystal Dragon and Order of the Scribes Wizard, known for being so tough to kill he singlehandedly ended the practice of promotion through Dead Wizards Curly Shoes.
 
The Chair of Abjuration and Runes, is a thin dwarfish man often mistaken for a gnome with a bushy beard, **Diedrich Bristlebroom**, travels the halls in his magical light construct, which resembles a sphere with magical armored legs and arms, almost like a battle platform walking through the halls of the university.
 
The Chair of Conjuration and Magical Creatures **Ahnkn**  
**asht the Unconquerable** is a Beholder with a fondness for small furry creatures, which he keeps in his office and classrooms.
 
The Chair of Divination and Astrology, **Dämmerung the Giant of Telenast**, is an Eclipse Giant and senior member of the board, dating back to the founding of Telenast, and is the reason for the Cathedral-Like scale of the University, as it needs to be big enough for him to move throughout the halls. Oddly enough, while Dämmerung is known to have a preternatural ability to predict events, he likens that to an advanced Mathematics formula rather than magic, this has sparked debate as to exactly what the difference really is.
 
The Chair of Enchantment and Aventure Studies **Daring Danger**, is seemingly a handsome and charming human man, and known Expeditioner into Colomb Bith. Daring is a master of the Enchantment school, and even memos containing his name often Magically rewrite themselves to highlight his bravery and debonair.
 
The Chair of Evocation and Magical History **Eiryn** is an Ancient Elf who has been at the school for the last 100 years, treating this position as a minor aside for an otherwise long journey. Eiryn is known for her consistent tardiness to meetings and classes as she cannot conceive of why wasting a day would be undesirable.
