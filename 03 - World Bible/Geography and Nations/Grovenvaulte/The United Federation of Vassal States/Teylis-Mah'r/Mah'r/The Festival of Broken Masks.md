600 years ago, before proud Teylis and ancient [[Mah'r]] joined to one city, when Teylis was even settled, Mah’r was beset by a great Dragon: the Fire Tyrant. It had perched atop Shimmering Peak (the upper part of the city), and declared Mah’r its domain. And there it rules, uncontested, demanding the taxes, labor, and occasionally bodies of the oldest city for 100 years. Atop the shimmering spire it amassed its riches in gold as well as bodies, its hoard growing fat off of the labor off the labor and servitude of the Mah’rish.  
Until the mid autumn, the second week of the Opal month a team known as the Broken Mask rose and raised its lair, and it fought back. By morning break the red light cast on Amber flame which consumes half the city, but in the middle the Flame Tyrant laid dead.  
And every year on the anniversary of death of the Flame Tyrant, people don broken masks in their honor, and a festival is held in their name: The Festival of the Broken Mask.
