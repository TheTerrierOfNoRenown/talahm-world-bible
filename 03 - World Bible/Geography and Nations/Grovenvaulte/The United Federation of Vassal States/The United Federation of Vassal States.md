#USVS

The United Federation of Vassal States is a set of 18 Nations, formerly Vassal states, united under one Federal Government; Located on the Westernmost coast of Grovenvaulte. Formed 98 years ago after the Vassal Rebellion, breaking away from the Uhterhest Empire. The UFVS is a Constitutional Republic, the a First Custodian who is elected every 8 years, A Parliament broken into two houses: The Parliament of Alderman and the Peoples Counsel, and the High Court, all housed in the capital Teylis-Mah'r.
