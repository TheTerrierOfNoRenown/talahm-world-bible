Colomb Bith is an ancient Elven civilization, mainly located off of the landbridge connecting Telara to Valustra that faced a catastrophe called "The Shattering" in DL 1135. Legends have it that it was an advanced civilization that fell due to its own hubris, simultaniously taking out its capital and magically killing 90% of the Entire Elven species, even those not within the bound of the city of Colomb Bith itself. Some say it was an experimental spell gone wrong, some say it was the Scattered God punishing them for attempting Godhood, some say they just phased into the Fae. Not even the surviving Elves know quite what happened, or at least aren't saying.
 
The Shattering shattered reality itself, and the ruins of the city are contained within a 100 Mile sphere of wild magic, pushing away the ocean in a perpetual "Sea crater", chucks of the original island floating in the sky, and rapidly changing weather in terrain, the ruins of Colomb Bith are the most unpredicatble place on Talahm.
