The few run ins that have been made with the administers of the Courts: The Fae, reveal that rather than be a species of people, the Faerie resembles more of a Class. \  
￼The Faerie Class has an unusual though all too familiar class structure. Many are born into it, denoted by silver cutlery as tongues (Born with a Silver Spoon in their Mouth). Though you will rarely see these, as Faerie tend to "Keep Up Appearances" by wearing succession of masks, especially in Court. It is rare that a Faerie is seen without their mask. Some social mobility exists, as it is possible to grab on to your footwear, and lift yourself three feet into the air to attain Faerie Status ("Pull yourself up by your Bootstraps).
 
A Marker of the Faerie Class is the cerulean ichor that constitutes their blood (Blue Bloods), this is a vital magical component to making a Faerie contract.
 
While many Faerie are long living, they are not immortal, rather many of their deals come with contracts dedicating "Years of Your Life" to them such that they are often "Living on Borrowed Time."
 
Faerie height is often variable, often viewed through most eyes as a trick of perspective. A Faerie that looks small may start approaching you so that they look bigger, as to best "Look down their noses at you."
 
Faerie are all powerful within their "Estate", the section of the Fae that they preside, but are often incapable of leaving said estate (the are "to the manor born")outside of specific times to which the monarchies of the court allow them to leave to visit their estate. Monarchs accomplish this by loudly yelling at animated arms that will then pass along the message ("A Call to Arms")
