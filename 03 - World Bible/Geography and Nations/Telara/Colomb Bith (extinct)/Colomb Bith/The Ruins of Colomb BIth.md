In the center of world, at the divide of Telara and Valustra there is a 200 Mile radius hole in the world. A tearing of the weave, a howling void where reality used to settle, a world where nonsense has taken the place of reality. This is what remains of the great city of Colomb Bith.
 
Colomb Bith, once the great metropolis of the Elves, disappeared during a violent and mysterious event called “The Shattering of Colomb Bith.” An event that took approximately 90% of the Elven population with it.
 
For the last few hundred years Expeditions into Colomb Bith have been undergone to try to ascertain what happened, and bring back artifacts from the advanced magical city.
