There are three main courts in the ruins of Colomb Bith.
 
1. The Court of Dreams - Associated with Fall. Governs Imagination and Whimsy. Believes in I constrained freedom. Deals tend to teach lessons, but are interpretable (like a dream)
2. Court of Ideas - Associated with Spring. Governs over Inspiration and Novelty. Believes in novelty and pursuing experience, in turning truth on its head -Deals come at immaterial costs. They take your favorite memory, or your best idea.
3. Court of Belief - Associated with Winter. Believes in order and rules, that everything must be governed by an underlying truth, whatever that may be. Governs that which you think is true and that which you wish for. Deals tend to be moral traps and punishments, or lessons.
4. Court of Feeling - Associated with Summer. Governs Emotion and Want. Believes in the instinctive satiation of desire and the need to throw oneself into feeling. Don’t deal, they take if certain conditions are met.
