The Broken Lantern is a bar and community hub for the [[The Purple Arches District]] neighborhood of [[Unterveil]]. Home and property of [[Brody Sloicht]], and his two daughters: [[Bailey Sloicht]] and [[Gretchen Sloicht]]. The Broken Lantern is described as the highest class bar in the [[Underdark]], which makes it a low-end dive by surface standards. 

The Broken Lantern is the primary watering hold for locals [[Shaver Cricks]] and [[Brayson]], who are friends with the owner.
