Unterveil is the [[Underdark]] city under [[Teylis-Mah'r]]. A [[Myconid]] colony, like most Underdark cities. Unterveil is the waste disposal system for Teylis-Mah'r, which filters most of its refuse and sewage through [[Briarbrook]]. This acts as a food source for the Myconid, who in turn do water treatment. This arrangement has served Unterveil and Teylis-Mah'r well for years, and in fact predates Teylis, being originally part of a treaty with Briarbrook and [[Mah'r]]. 



## Appearence

The immediate smell of Unterveil is a sterile, almost stale air as all contaminants are filtered by the Myconid. The buildings look like grown mushrooms, because they basically are. They are Myconid, who are grown to the desired shape and accommodation for the Denizens of Unterveil. The city is densely packed and grown on top of itself, growing from the cavern floor to the ceiling which stands an incredible 150 feet high at its highest point. Several fluorescent mushrooms grow from the ceiling and off of building providing light to its cobble streets, which cover the [[Mycelial Network]].

## Infrastructure and Economy

Like most Undercities, Unterveil traffics in two things: Waste and Crime. Waste is the primary function. [[Teylis-Mah'r]] pumps its sewage and waste into Unterveil via its [[Pumphouse]], it is then funneled into the city using a network of [[Rot Houses]] that feed the Myconid population. In exchange the Myconid pump back clean water as a byproduct of their feeding and filtering. This arrangement is the prime cause of treaties between the overworld and the [[Underdark]]. As a result this makes Unterveil the perfect place for criminal organizations.

Crimes families, such as [[The Rosa Family]] use the fact that the [[Unterveil Police Department]] is notoriously underfunded, the fact that most do not like to look where their sewage goes, and the general ambivalence of the [[Myconid]], to traffic weapons, finance, and drugs. 

As a result of all of this, the people who live in Unterveil do not live there out of choice, but rather because they have no other place to go. The conditions in Unterveil are notoriously poor, and merchants at the [[Unterveil Bazaar]] are often understocked, though word has it that if you fall in the good graces of some of the merchants, they may be able to point you to the [[Untermarket]].

## History

The exact origin of Unterveil is unknown, originally a [[Drow]] colony, Unterveil was left abandoned after most Drow disappeared following [[The Shattering of Colomb Bith]]. Most useful artifacts were either pillaged or destroyed during the [[Undersucession Wars]] that followed the Drow disappearance. While the [[Duergar]], [[Derro]], and [[Svirfneblin]] warred with the [[Illithid]], the [[Myconid]] grew in number and strength until late Opal DL 1457 a Myconid by the name of [[Speaker]] first emerged with the unique trait to communicate with what he referred to as [[Animal Kind]]. 

Speaker would negotiate a deal with the surface world that they would send their refuse and sewage to the Myconid, and in return the Myconid would clean the water and prevent the [[Undersucession Wars]] from spilling over into the surface. By 16 Garnet, DL 1458 The Treaty of Rot was signed in Unterveil, establishing it as a new city connected to [[Fort Cape]], [[Briarbrook]], and [[Mah'r]]. This treaty would be expanded to [[Teylis-Mah'r]] following [[Sovereign Day]] on 12 Garnet, DL 1628.

## Government and Politics

The exacts government of Unterveil is opaque to most people, as it is determined by the [[Mycelial Network]], through methods known only to the [[Myconid]]. Myconid, who are a hivemind species, see little need in organizational structures like government, and instead follow higher order organizational structures. 

What is known is each Myconid is grown for specific tasks, and pursue those tasks at the exclusion of all others. Some tasks are to become housing for the [[Animal Kind]], others infrastructure maintenance, others are to maintain the rot house, and others [[Speaker|Speakers]], named after the first Speaker, who can communicate with other species.

Apart from them, as part of the treaty, [[Teylis-Mah'r]] supplies funding and oversight for the [[Unterveil Police Department]], to manage the Animal Kind population of Unterveil.
