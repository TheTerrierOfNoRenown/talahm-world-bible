Pellnast Sailborn Coinminter, also known as the Crimson King, was a fascist political agitator in the latter end of the 1600s. First starting as a popular figure among the Gnomish Elites, his ideology of [[Founder Supremacy]] resonated among the upper class and the gentry in [[The United Federation of Vassal States]]. Before long Coinminter had gained a massive following in the South West of the UFVS, winning a governorship in [[Grartum]] in DL 1697. 

Benefitting from an economic boon at the turn of the 18th Century, due mostly the to continued industrialization of the UFVS, Coinminter became incredibly popular as a governor in the South West, with even mentions of run at become [[Custodian]] of the UFVS. But Coinminter's ambitions were grander. Coinminter did not believe in Democracy, often calling it a "scourge", that enforced the lesser, non-founder races (specifically the Orcs) political power, and believed thoroughly into the return to Monarchy. 

## THe start of the The [[The Red Rebellion]] 
![[05 - Resources and Reference/Pictures/Character Pictures/Notable Figures/The Crimson King.png|right|400]]
On 02 Ruby DL 1702, Coinminter unveiled The Crimson King. Which would be both his name, and his battle platform. A towering monstrosity of a battle mech that required over 300 on board staff to operate. This was the first move in [[The Red Rebellion]], the campaign that would eventually start [[The Great War]].
