On 02 Sapphire DL 1702 [[Pellnast Sailborn Coinminter|The Crimson King]] was unveiled, beginning of The Summer of Ruby Flame, and the start of the Red Rebellion. Led by [[Pellnast Sailborn Coinminter|The Crimson King]], the Red Rebellion was a fascist reactionary campaign, hell bent on installing [[Pellnast Sailborn Coinminter|The Crimson King]] as the new monarch of all of [[Grovenvaulte]]. The Red Rebellion descended all of [[Talahm]] into a World War known as [[The Great War]].

The Red Rebellion started with three states: [[Gratrum]], [[Ykssaj]], and [[Ronde]]. By the end of the war eight states had joined the Crimson Army in [[The Founders Accord]] is formed, transforming a civil war in [[The Great War]].

## The March of the Crimson King

The first major campaign involved the new mobile platform named The Crimson King, after the alias of Coinminter. The first campaign of the war involved the Crimson Army accompanying the Platform North East toward [[Teylis-Mah'r]], They would meet in the [[Billowing Pasture]] in [[New Chesterwick]], This would become the first major battlefield in [[The Great War]]. 

The March of the Crimson King continued after the Battle of Billowing Pasture was won on Opal 3, DL 1704; 2 years, 6 months, and 12 days (946 days) after the beginning of the war, following the unveiling the [[Construct]] and the [[First Construct Battalion]]. 

The March of the Crimson King would continue starting and stopping and being interrupted by ongoing battles until 22 Emerald, DL 1713.

## The construct army

On Opal 3, DL 1704 the [[First Construct Battalion]] was unveiled at the [[Battle of Billowing Pasture]], bringing a decisive end to the longest campaign in the war in 36 hours. After this the Red Rebellion boasted the most formidable force in war: the [[Construct]]. This key advantage is what lead to the formation of [[Grovenvaulte Alliance]] and [[The Founders Accord]]. This would be the catalyst for the descent into [[The Great War]].

The Construct Army was key to the success of The Red Rebellion, and is widely considered to be the reason the war lasted as long as it did. This advantage lasted until 02 Sapphire DL 1710, when [[The Construct Awakening]] too place.
